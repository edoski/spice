EVM fee glossary

The minimum vocabulary needed to reason about priority fees without confusing work, price, limits, and total payment.

TermMeaningUnit
Gas usedComputation and storage work consumed by one transaction or block.gas
Gas limitMaximum gas the transaction authorizes, or maximum gas the block can hold.gas
Base feeProtocol-set minimum price per gas for the block.wei/gas
Priority feeExtra price per gas offered for priority.wei/gas
Effective gas priceActual base fee plus actual priority fee paid per gas.wei/gas
Transaction feeGas used multiplied by effective gas price.wei
Max priority feeSender's cap on the priority-fee component.wei/gas
Max feeSender's cap on base fee plus priority fee.wei/gas
Block median priority feeEffective priority fee below or equal to which half of the block's consumed gas fell.wei/gas
transaction fee = gas used × (base fee per gas + effective priority fee per gas)

Chain distinction

Ethereum and Polygon direct priority fees to the block producer. Avalanche C-Chain burns both base and priority fees, but still uses priority fees for ordering. User cost remains gas used multiplied by base plus priority fee.

Primary references: Ethereum gas and fees, Polygon EIP-1559, and Avalanche fees.
