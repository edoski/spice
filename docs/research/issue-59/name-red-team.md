# Issue 59: bounded name red team

**Status: approved decision evidence.** Edo finally confirmed **FABLE — Fee Analysis
through Blockchain Learning and Estimation** after this bounded challenge. Checked
2026-07-15. This note authorizes no renaming or implementation. The owner-accepted F#
Fable compiler and small Ethereum AI-token collisions are intentionally excluded.

## Recommendation

Retain **FABLE**. Kairos wins only on the narrow metaphor of an opportune moment. FABLE
wins the whole boundary: it reads cleanly, its approved expansion states the actual thesis
scope, and `fable` supports the already-approved single active technical stem. The
fiction/falsehood sense is real but bounded by the mandatory first-mention expansion.

Do not create mythological backronyms, alternate technical stems, aliases, or compatibility
names. If public PyPI publication later becomes a requirement, treat registry ownership as
a separate release-channel constraint; do not silently weaken this naming decision.
